Quick Start

Last updated: Jun 22th, 2017

Overview

Kajabi is a platform that contains all the features you need to sell, market and deliver your content. This training guide provides tutorials, examples, and links for setting up and maintaining your Kajabi campaign.

If you cannot find your answer on this guide, you can email us at support@schoolofonlinebusiness.com. You can also type your question into Kajabi's Help Center, but remember that you agreed that you will NOT contact Kajabi's support team. If you do so, you understand that you may have your account with us terminated and this agreement canceled.

If you want the ability to contact Kajabi support, you should enroll with your own Kajabi account and not enroll into this build-it-for-you-program, so you can build the technical aspects of your funnel on your own with Kajabi's support desk assisting.

Step Two - Creating Your First Landing Page

Some parts of Kajabi have been updated since this video. See updated written steps below.

Step 2: Creating Your First Landing Page – Kajabi - Kajabi Help Center

Steps for Creating a Landing Page
  1. Under Marketing, click Landing Pages.
  2. Click the blue +New Landing Page button at the top right.
  3. Choose a template (we recommend starting with Standard Premier).
    • Click the blue Get Started button.
    • Type a title for your Site and click Create.
  4. Click section titles on the left to edit content.
  5. When finished, click the blue Save button.

Step Three - Creating Your First Product or Course

Step 3: Creating Your First Product or Course – Kajabi - Kajabi Help Center

Steps for Creating a Product or Course
  1. Under Marketing, click Products.
  2. Click the blue +New Product button at the top right.
  3. Add a title and a description. Click Save.
  4. Click Change Thumbnail, choose photo, and Save (if you have a branding image to upload).
  5. Click the Post tab to add content for your customers.
    • Click the blue +New Post button at the top right.
    • Give the post a Title, Category, and Body text
    • If you have a video and image, use the upload buttons
    • Save (for the post to go live, change Visibility to Published).

Step Four - Sell Your First Product or Course

Creating an Offer and a Checkout Page

Step 4: Sell Your First Product or Course – Kajabi - Kajabi Help Center

  1. Under Store, click Sales.
    • Later, we will cover how to connect with Stripe to receive payments.
  2. Click the Offers tab.
  3. Click the blue +Add New Offer button at the top right.
  4. Complete the fields and click Save.
  5. Choose which product the customer should have access to upon purchasing
  6. Click Preview to see the page before going live

Step Five - Setting Up Your First Site

Step 5: Setting Up Your First Site – Kajabi - Kajabi Help Center

  1. Under the Marketing tab, click Website.
  2. Click the Appearance tab.
  3. To edit your site, hover over your theme image and click Customize (or click the Customize icon beneath the image).
  4. Use the various fields to edit any part of your site
    • Be sure to change out the default branding and logos.
  5. Click Save when finished.
  6. Under the Marketing tab, click Settings to find your Subdomain
    • To set up a custom domain, start here.
